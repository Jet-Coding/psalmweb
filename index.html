Reach The Campus, Transform The World.

Raising Student Leaders for Christ since 1969

PSALM is a campus and marketplace ministry that envisions to produce dynamic servant leaders committed to transforming the world.

It was established on August 8, 1969 as College Youth Center (CYC) in Zamboanga City through Dr. Joseph Arthur.
